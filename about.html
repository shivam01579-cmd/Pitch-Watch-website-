About Pitch Watch

Last Updated: May 31, 2026

Welcome to Pitch Watch — Your Premium Destination for All Things Cricket.

At Pitch Watch, we are passionate about the game of cricket. Our mission is to deliver comprehensive, high-quality, and real-time coverage of cricket matches, trends, and analyses. Whether it is an edge-of-the-seat IPL thriller, a historic Test match battle, or an international ODI clash, we bring you the stories that matter from around the cricketing world.

What We Cover

We provide extensive coverage across major cricketing domains:

  • Indian Premier League (IPL): Ball-by-ball analyses, qualifiers, highlights, team previews, player auctions, and stats.
  • International Cricket (ICC Tournaments): Extensive reporting on the T20 World Cup, ODI World Cup, World Test Championship, and bilateral series.
  • Team India & Domestic Cricket: In-depth coverage of Ranji Trophy, Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy, Duleep Trophy, and domestic talent news.
  • Behind-the-Scenes & Analytics: Selection dynamics, expert opinions, player injury updates, transfers, controversies, and tactical shifts.

Our Promise

We believe in integrity, accuracy, and depth. Our dedicated team of writers and sports analysts work round the clock to ensure every report, statistical breakdown, and opinion piece is fully researched, fact-checked, and presented in an easy-to-read, engaging format. Thank you for joining us on this cricketing journey!
